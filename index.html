How it works

One dive, one post.

On DIVR, logging your dive is how you share it. No separate "compose post" — just log it once, and the whole story goes with you.

01

Fill in the dive

Depth, bottom time, temperature, visibility, gas, gear — log the numbers a diver actually cares about. Add a few photos and pin the site.

02

Set who sees it

Public for the wider community, just your followers, or private to your profile. The same log entry, however you want to share it.

03

Hit post

Your dive lands in feeds instantly — stats and all. No double-entering data, no juggling apps. The log is the post, and the post is the log.

Bring your history

Every dive you've ever done.

Years of dives sitting in a paper logbook? Snap a photo of each page and our AI reads it for you — site names, depths, dates, bottom times, the lot. Every old dive lands in your profile, ready to share.
